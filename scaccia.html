SCACCIA

When my parents got married, they struck a deal. The kids would be raised Jewish, in my mother’s tradition, as long as she agreed to prepare scaccia- a local delicacy from my grandparent’s Sicilian hometown- every Christmas. My mother has followed through for- most of :) -thirty-one years. Grandma, Poppa, Nonna, and Nonno were all happy, and my life has been enriched by a celebration of both cultures.

Nonna, the matriarch, took the reins this year.
Dad: "You want me to do it now?"
Nonna: "If you don't ruin it"
CLASSIC NONNA! Got to love her!
