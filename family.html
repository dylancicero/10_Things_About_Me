FAMILY LORE

Family lore: My great-grandfather sold fruit in the early 1900s on the Coney- Island boardwalk with his good friend, Nathan Handwerker. Nathan proposed one day that they switch from selling fruit to selling hot dogs, to which my great- grandfather responded, “That's the stupidest idea I've ever heard! At least with fruit and vegetables, your family will never be hungry!” ... Nathan went it alone and “Nathan’s Famous Hot Dogs” was born. Who could've guessed?
