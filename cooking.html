COOKING

I love to cook. Some of favorite dishes are chickpea stew , Moosewood pad thai , and homemade pasta! Recently, I've enjoyed trying recipes from Yotam Ottolenghi's Jerusalem cookbook. The pureed beets with yogurt and za'atar is absolutely incredible! I also love to experiment with exotic dishes from wild-foraged foods- I make a mean dandelion pesto , beer-battered milkweed tempura, and oyster mushroom chowder . Mangia!
