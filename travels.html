COUCHSURFING

In 2016, I road-tripped from New York to California and back, traveling to and from a (sustainable) farming apprenticeship. Along the way, I "couchsurfed" in the homes of dozens of strangers in cities across America and befriended some extraordinary people. Most memorable, there was Paul from San Rafael, CA- a seventy- year-old hippie, now confined to his rocking chair, who shared his experiences hitching to Grateful Dead shows. And there was Chuck, from El Paso, TX- a high school sign language teacher and wrestling coach who refused to allow his disability (he was completely deaf) to get in the way of a “normal” life. I will always remember the kindness and hospitality of these people.
